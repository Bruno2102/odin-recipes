Chicken fillet roll

Description

Chicken fillet roll is favorite meal of every Irish, and it's ingredients are fairly simple. Take a roll, cut it, put butter or mayo, cut chicken fillet in smaller pieces, arrange it in a roll, add favorite salats and voila!

Ingredients
  • White roll
  • Chicken fillet
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
    • Steps
    1. Cut the roll
    2. Cut chicken fillet into smaller pieces
    3. Arrange chicken fillet in roll
